Introduction

Exam Warriors is a book by Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi, published in 2018. Exam Warriors is written for young students to help them deal with the stress of exams.

Other details

The new and enhanced edition of Exam Warriors by Narendra Modi is not only an inspiring book for students but also for their parents and teachers. Written in a fun and interactive style, with illustrations, activities and yoga asanas, this book will be a friend in acing exams and facing life.Non-preachy, practical and thought-provoking, Exam Warriors is a handy guide for youth of India and across the world.
